A Quirky Romanian Artist 

My name is Cassandra Calin. I am a comic artist, illustrator and graphic designer. I am based in Montreal, Canada. I have graduated from Dawson College with a College Studies Diploma in Graphic Design. I like 90’s music, playing the piano, cartoons, pineapple and cats. I like cats a lot. I currently run a blog where I publish illustrative work and comic art inspired by my life and experiences.If you wish to contact me, feel free to do so via e-mail at cassiecalin@gmail.com
